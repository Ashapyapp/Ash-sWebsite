Things I Use

I don't use anything expensive. I have an old HP Pavilion dv6 laptop, which I've upgraded with some new parts to make it more usable.

  • I added a 4GB DDR3 1800 RAM module from my old Lenovo laptop, which surprisingly worked very well.
  • I replaced the hard disk with an SSD, as I noticed the disk usage was unreasonably high. I now use a 128GB SSD.

As for operating systems, I used to be a big fan of Windows, but recently I switched to Kali Linux. I love the terminal in Linux and I'm preparing to switch to a Mac soon.
